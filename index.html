The Benefits of Canine & Feline Massage

At Dash Veterinary Clinic, we offer specialized massage therapy designed specifically for dogs and cats. Our therapeutic massage techniques provide numerous benefits for your pet's health and wellbeing.

  • Reduces pain and inflammation
  • Improves circulation and flexibility
  • Accelerates recovery from injuries
  • Decreases anxiety and stress
  • Enhances mobility in older pets
  • Strengthens the bond between pet and owner

Our massage therapy sessions are tailored to each pet's specific needs, whether they're recovering from surgery, managing arthritis, or simply need relaxation.

Our Approach to Animal Massage

Dr. George Dash has specialized training in animal massage therapy and combines his veterinary expertise with gentle, effective massage techniques.

Each session begins with a thorough assessment of your pet's condition, followed by a customized treatment plan that may include:

Swedish Massage

Gentle techniques to promote relaxation and improve circulation.

Deep Tissue Therapy

Targeted pressure to release chronic muscle tension and knots.

Passive Range of Motion

Gentle movements to maintain joint flexibility and mobility.

Whether your pet is an active athlete or a senior companion, massage therapy can significantly enhance their quality of life.
